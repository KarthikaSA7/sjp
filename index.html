‘The Impact Of Asia On The Global Agenda’

Join a panel of distinguished speakers from around the globe as they share their insights on the most powerful financial trend on the planet: Asia’s rapid growth and rising global influence.

Asia’s economic, environmental and geopolitical influence are shaping the world.

Naturally, Asia’s rising influence is having an impact on the regional and international investment landscape.

This means for those looking to build and protect wealth through this exciting period of dynamic change, knowledge and insight is vital.

That’s why St. James’s Place is bringing a panel of world-renowned financial and economic experts to share their views with a live audience.

St. James’s Place is a FTSE-100 wealth management business with offices in Hong Kong, Singapore and across Mainland China.

We are one of Asia's leading wealth management companies.

Discover the trends shaping the investment landscape in 2020 and beyond

The 2019 Asia Investment Conference aims to give you a broad overview of where global markets are at as we approach a new decade starting in 2020.

Our speakers will share their sharp, expert insights into international investment trends, Asia’s growing influence in the global economy and the rise of sustainability and its impact on investing.

Our Investment Committee Chairman, David Lamb, will then bring these big-picture themes into his keynote presentation about what it means for you.

We’ll wrap our conference with a Q&A session followed by complimentary drinks once our panel has finished speaking.
